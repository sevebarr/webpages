Building New Homes Since 1985

At Oranssi, we are more than just a construction company; we are your trusted partner in turning dreams into reality. We have been a Sole Proprietorship for 28 years with a strong commitment to excellence, we have built a reputation for delivering top-tier construction and renovation services. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create spaces that reflect our clients' aspirations and exceed their expectations. What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and client satisfaction. Our team of highly skilled professionals brings a wealth of experience to every project, ensuring precision in every detail. From crafting bespoke additions that seamlessly blend with your existing structure to creating outdoor spaces that invite you to embrace the beauty of nature, we take pride in our ability to turn concepts into breathtaking realities. At Oranssi, we don't just build structures; we build relationships, trust, and lasting impressions. Join us on this journey, and let's build your vision together.
